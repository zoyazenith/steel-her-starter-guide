The Workout
YOUR 10-MINUTE STARTER CIRCUIT
5 exercises. Do all 5 back to back = 1 round. Rest 60 seconds. Repeat for 2 rounds total.
⏱ The Format

Do each exercise for the reps listed → move straight to the next with no rest between exercises → rest 60 seconds after all 5 → repeat for Round 2. Total time: approximately 10 minutes.

SQUAT

Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out. Sit back and down keeping chest tall. Lower until thighs are as parallel to floor as you can manage. Drive through heels to stand.

Key: Knees push outward as you rise — never cave inward.
PUSH-UP
Beginner
Chest · Arms · Shoulders · Core
8
Reps
2
Rounds
0s
Rest after

Hands shoulder-width, body in a straight line. Lower chest toward floor, elbows at 45 degrees. Push back up. Drop to knees if needed — that is a valid modification, not a weakness.

Key: Quality beats quantity. 5 perfect reps beat 15 sloppy ones.
GLUTE BRIDGE
Beginner
Glutes · Hamstrings · Lower back
12
Reps
2
Rounds
0s
Rest after

Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat on floor. Press through heels and lift hips up. Squeeze your glutes hard at the top for 1 full second. Lower with control. Do not let hips touch the floor between reps.

Key: The 1-second squeeze at the top is where the glute activation happens. Don't skip it.

On forearms and toes, hold your body in a perfectly straight line from head to heels. Squeeze your core and glutes tight. Breathe steadily. Do not let hips drop or rise.

Key: 20 seconds of perfect tension beats 60 seconds of sagging hips. Build time each week.

High plank position. Drive one knee toward chest then quickly switch. Keep hips level — no bouncing. This is your cardio finisher for each round. Go at a pace you can sustain with control.

Key: After this exercise rest 60 seconds, then go back to squats for Round 2.
